covalent Case Study

Timeline

10 days

Platform

Digital

My Role

Logo Designer

Logo design for ERP system

For this project I was contracted to create a new logo for Covalent, a modern Enterprise Resource Planning system challenging the status quo.

Described as "The ultimate ERP that grows with your business by providing quick setup, business needs scaling and increased intelligence per user."

The target audience for Covalent includes B2B SaaS tech companies looking to improve their business efficiency.

Introduction

After an initial interview with the client, I was able to discover their vision and particular tastes they had that would contribute to the logo design.

They favoured descriptors of "Reliable, modern, cutting-edge, innovative" for their brand. When asked about what attributes they would like their target audience to think of when they looked at their logo, they mentioned reliability, flexibility and intelligence.

The client had an initial idea of the business logo representing a "covalent bond" found in science but used symbolically to show the connections between the different components of an ERP system. I tried to keep this idea in mind and see how far I could push this concept.

Requests for a logo mark and simple font were made alongside a monochrome colour scheme.

Regular feedback loop

After each significant stage of ideation, feedback was regularly sought after and communicated. For this stage of concepts, the client considered the various explorations and chose to develop the first concept further.

Final Design

For the final design, I retained the initial idea of a "covalent bond" but also incorporated the business descriptors of "cutting edge", "modern", and "innovative" with the incorporation of the sharp angular tails.

A web-safe and flexible font of DM Sans was selected as the primary font for Covalent, alongside a monochromatic colour palette.
