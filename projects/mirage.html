Logo design for new lifestyle app

Mirage is a lifestyle app (mobile) that provides personalised text and multimedia community posts. A reliable source of life hacks, travel tips, current events and reviews delivered through an encouraging and positive environment. I was contracted to create a new logo for this developing app that would solidify its branding and stand out amongst similar apps on the marketplace.

The client wanted to use a blue-magenta palette to signify forward-thinking, creativity, and intelligence.

We explored twelve distinct marks split across three core creative pillars: fluid topography, fractured grids, and mirrored typography.

Using the blue-magenta base for the colour scheme, experimentation with colour palettes was used to explore possibilities.
